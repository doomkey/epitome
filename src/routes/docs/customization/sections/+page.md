# Sections

Epitome allows you to hide/rename/reorder the sections in your resume.

**Reordering the Personal section is not possible.** (why would you even do that?)

<img src={se} />


## Hiding and Reordering Sections

> Menu > Customization > Configure Section.

Click the eye icon to toggle visibility which is on the left side of a section title.

To change the order of a section, click the up/down arrow in the right side.

## Renaming Section

Click the pencil icon next to the title of the section. Edit and then press enter/click the tick to save.
